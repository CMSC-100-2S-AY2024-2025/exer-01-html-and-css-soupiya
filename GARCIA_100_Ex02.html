Projects



The "Diet Problem Solver" aims to find the cheapest and most nutritious combination of foods that will satisfy all the daily nutritional requirements of an individual. The problem is formulated as a linear program where the objective is to minimize cost and meet constraints but will still satisfy the nutritional needs.



"Passenger Rush" is a local two-player game played on a single computer, where both players take on the role of jeepney drivers with default routes on Kaliwa and Kanan. The objective is to transport as many passengers as possible to the Junction Unloading Area while avoiding natural and user-made disasters.



"Plants and Pests" is a decision-making game engine where players assume the role of a farmer managing 16 different farm lots. The game aims to test your strategic thinking and quick decision-making skills as you navigate the dynamic challenges of agricultural management.
